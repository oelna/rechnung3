Table formula examples

Start formulas with =. Cell references use Excel-like columns and row numbers such as A1, B2, and D4.

  • =B1*C1 multiplies two cells.
  • =A4 mirrors another cell's displayed value.
  • =SUM(D1:D4,B1,C3,D3) sums ranges and individual cells.
  • =SUM(A4) sums one cell.
  • =ROUND(B1*C1, 2) rounds to two decimals.
